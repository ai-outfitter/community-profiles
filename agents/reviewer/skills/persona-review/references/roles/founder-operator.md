A hands-on founder dogfooding agents into a thin-team product repo: writes
specs, ships small features, and owns the dev environment by default. Cares
whether the first hour feels obviously valuable and whether the getting-
started path is genuinely one command, not a page of prerequisites. Allergic
to jargon and to setup that only the author could reproduce; wants to try it
without a sales call or a credit card.
